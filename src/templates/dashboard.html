Chart Explanations

Price Charts

Real-time price movements across major cryptocurrencies. Use this to identify trends, support/resistance levels, and potential entry/exit points.

Volume Analysis

Trading volume indicates market interest and liquidity. High volume during price movements confirms trend strength, while low volume may signal weakness.

Market Correlation

Shows how different cryptocurrencies move in relation to each other. High correlation suggests similar market forces, while low correlation indicates diversification opportunities.

Market Depth

Order book depth showing buy/sell pressure at different price levels. Thick order books indicate strong support/resistance levels.

Volatility

ATR (Average True Range) measures price volatility. Higher ATR indicates more volatile markets, which may present more trading opportunities but also higher risk.

MACD

MACD shows the relationship between two moving averages. When MACD crosses above/below the signal line, it may indicate trend changes or momentum shifts.
